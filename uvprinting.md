## Image Edit

1. Tools unblur / remove bg of image
   - https://picwish.com/unblur-image-portrait
   - https://www.remove.bg/

2. Photoshop
   - Width / Height at least need 1000px
   <img src="uvprinting/img05.png" width="100%" style="display:block; max-width:400px;" />

   - Brightness + 30 to 40%
   <img src="uvprinting/img06.png" width="100%" style="display:block; max-width:400px;" />

   - <b>** Must save in jpg format **</b>

3. RIIN
   - Print canva: 16Pass 4C 2bit textile
   - Safe size: 100×100mm (Standard leaf size)
   - Import jpg
   <img src="uvprinting/img07.png" width="100%" style="display:block; max-width:200px;" />

   - Save in .prn format and ready to print
